My cat has been losing a lot of weight recently, even though her diet hasn't changed. What might be the problem?

Has your cat been shedding more pounds than hair lately? Is her rich fur coat looking dull? These could be signs of roundworm.

Roundworms are spaghetti-like parasites around three to five inches long that may appear in Fluffy's feces. These uninvited guests take out a temporary lease on your cat's intestine, where they steal some of kitty's chow before it is digested.
